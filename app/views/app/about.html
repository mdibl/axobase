Who We Are

We are international group of researchers that are committed to empowerment of axolotl and other salamanders for research purposes. For the last three years, our community has gathered to hold meetings where we not only exchange scientific progress, but also discuss issues that are necessary for the progress of the field.

History of Meetings

  1. 1st Salamander meeting was held at Research – Institute of Molecular Pathology (IMP), Vienna in 2018
  2. 2nd Salamander meeting was held at Northeastern University, Boston in 2019
  3. The 3rd edition of the meeting for 2020 was postponed due to the COVID-19 Pandemic.
  4. 3rd Salamander meeting will be held at Center for Regenerative Therapies (CRTD), TU Dresden in 2021. (link)
